The best movies according to Ananya

My top 3 movies of all time.

Interstellar

This is an amazing sci-fi movie. Watched it at least 5 times and it always amazes me.

The Pursuit of Happiness

This is also an amazing movie and tells what a human being goes through in order to achieve happiness. Happiness in little things.

The Dark Knight

Another masterpiece by Christopher Nolan. Loved the story and how the human mind works in different situations. A must watch for all.
